Jackie Liu (she/her) is a 19-year-old figurative artist from the Greater Boston area studying Art Practice and Philosophy at Stanford University. Her oil and acrylic paintings serve as intimate memoir-like vignettes, traversing themes of trauma, identity, and resilience. By articulating and sharing her own stories, she aims to celebrate vulnerability, foster communion, and invite conversation around the stigmatized facets of the human experience.
